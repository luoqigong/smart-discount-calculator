Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Smart Discount Calculator is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Calculation Data: Your discount calculations are processed locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our application (anonymized data only).
  • Local Storage: Your preferences and recent calculations may be stored locally in your browser for convenience.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and improve our discount calculation services
  • Understand usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Maintain and optimize application performance
  • Ensure the security and reliability of our service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your calculation data remains on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any data we do collect:

  • All calculations are performed client-side in your browser
  • No personal financial information is transmitted to our servers
  • We use industry-standard security practices for our web application
  • Any analytics data is anonymized and aggregated

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding user behavior (with IP anonymization)
  • Content Delivery Networks: To ensure fast loading times
  • Hosting Services: To provide reliable access to our application

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Remember your calculation preferences
  • Store recent calculations for your convenience
  • Maintain your preferred settings

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all locally stored calculation data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Request information about any data we may have collected
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your usage

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
